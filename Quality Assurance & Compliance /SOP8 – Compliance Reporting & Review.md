#  **SOP 8 – Compliance Reporting & Review**

---

## **1. Purpose**

To establish a **standardized, transparent, and actionable compliance reporting process** that:

* Tracks organizational adherence to policies, regulations, and contractual obligations.
* Provides management with timely insights to enable informed decisions.
* Supports audit readiness and continuous compliance improvement.

---

## **2. Objectives**

* Standardize compliance report format, frequency, and ownership.
* Monitor CAPA (Corrective & Preventive Actions) progress.
* Identify trends in non-compliance and potential risks.
* Facilitate management review and strategic action planning.

---

## **3. Scope**

Applies to:

* All departments engaged in compliance activities.
* All compliance areas: policy adherence, quality checks, data protection, and regulatory requirements.
* Internal, external, client audits, and regulatory reporting cycles.

---

## **4. Roles & Responsibilities**

| Role                                      | Responsibility                                              |
| ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Compliance Officer / Manager**          | Compile data, validate, and create reports.                 |
| **Department Heads**                      | Submit accurate compliance data on time.                    |
| **QA / Audit Teams**                      | Provide audit findings and trend analysis.                  |
| **Legal Officer**                         | Report regulatory changes affecting compliance.             |
| **Top Management / Compliance Committee** | Review dashboards, approve actions, and allocate resources. |

---

## **5. Frequency of Reporting**

| Report Type                        | Frequency | Owner                 | Audience                    |
| ---------------------------------- | --------- | --------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Weekly Snapshot                    | Weekly    | Compliance Officer    | Department Heads            |
| Monthly Summary                    | Monthly   | Compliance Manager    | Department Heads, Ops Leads |
| Quarterly Compliance Review Report | Quarterly | Compliance Manager    | Top Management              |
| Annual Compliance Report           | Annual    | Director – Compliance | Board / Audit Committee     |

---

## **6. End-to-End Workflow Process**

### **Phase 1 – Data Collection**

1. Collect compliance data from each department using **standard templates**.
2. Include:

   * Policy adherence checks
   * CAPA updates
   * Audit results
   * Regulatory change implementation status
3. Verify accuracy and completeness.

---

### **Phase 2 – Data Consolidation & Analysis**

1. Merge departmental data into a **central compliance database**.
2. Analyze:

   * Compliance percentage per department
   * CAPA closure trends
   * Recurring non-compliances
   * Risk exposure (financial, legal, operational)
3. Identify high-risk areas for management attention.

---

### **Phase 3 – Report Preparation**

1. Prepare reports with standard sections:

   * Executive Summary
   * Departmental Scorecards
   * Non-Compliance Findings & Trends
   * CAPA Status
   * Risk Assessment & Recommendations
2. Include **charts, dashboards, and visual indicators** for clarity.
3. Conduct peer review for accuracy.

---

### **Phase 4 – Management Review**

1. Present reports in **Quarterly Compliance Review Meetings (QCRM)**.
2. Discuss:

   * Compliance scores and trends
   * Open CAPA items and delays
   * High-risk departments or functions
   * Regulatory updates requiring attention
3. Record meeting minutes and action decisions.

---

### **Phase 5 – Corrective Action Follow-Up**

1. Assign responsibility for each approved action.
2. Track progress using the **CAPA Tracker**.
3. Escalate overdue or critical issues as per the escalation matrix.
4. Verify closure and effectiveness of actions.

---

### **Phase 6 – Documentation & Archival**

1. Store all reports, dashboards, CAPA logs, and review minutes in the **central repository**.
2. Maintain version control and restricted access.
3. Retain documents for **minimum 7 years** or as legally required.

## **8. KPIs**

| KPI                              | Target     | Measurement         |
| -------------------------------- | ---------- | ------------------- |
| On-time report submission        | 100%       | Compliance calendar |
| CAPA closure rate                | ≥ 95%      | CAPA tracker        |
| Non-compliance recurrence        | ≤ 5%       | Audit reports       |
| Management review attendance     | 100%       | Attendance sheet    |
| Time to escalate critical issues | ≤ 24 hours | Escalation log      |

## **9. Escalation Matrix**

| Severity | Escalation Path               | Timeframe          |
| -------- | ----------------------------- | ------------------ |
| Minor    | Department Head               | 5 working days     |
| Major    | Compliance Manager → Director | 48 hrs             |
| Critical | CEO / Board                   | Immediate (24 hrs) |

## **10. Tools & Systems**

* **Power BI / Tableau / Zoho Analytics:** Visual dashboards

* **Jira / Excel:** CAPA & tracking

---

## **11. Best Practices**

✅ Use dashboards to show **compliance % trends** clearly.
✅ Automate CAPA follow-up reminders.
✅ Correlate compliance metrics with **risk and operational impact**.
✅ Schedule quarterly reviews consistently.
✅ Link compliance KPIs to **departmental performance metrics**.
